About the Firm

Grand Prairie's Trusted Personal Injury & Civil Litigation Law Firm

Thorne & Skinner has been serving the Dallas–Fort Worth community for decades. Located in the heart of Grand Prairie, our firm combines decades of trial experience with a genuine commitment to our clients. We don't just handle your case. We fight for the outcome you deserve.

We represent individuals and families who have been seriously injured or lost a loved one due to someone else's negligence. Our attorneys are AV Preeminent® rated by Martindale-Hubbell, the highest possible peer-review rating for legal ability and ethical standards. It reflects the trust the legal community places in our firm.

In addition to litigation, our full-service firm handles real estate transactions, wills and estates, family law, and business matters. We also operate an in-house title company to serve clients end-to-end on real estate closings.

What We Handle

Practice Areas

From serious injury cases to real estate closings, Thorne & Skinner has the experience to guide you through your legal matter.

🚗

Personal Injury

Our primary focus. We aggressively pursue maximum compensation for victims of:

  • Car & truck accidents
  • 18-Wheeler / commercial truck crashes
  • Motorcycle accidents
  • Uber / Lyft rideshare accidents
  • Slip and fall / premises liability
  • Dog bites & animal attacks
  • Traumatic brain injuries (TBI)
  • Nursing home abuse & neglect
  • Dram shop / liquor liability
⚖️

Wrongful Death

When negligence takes a life, we stand with surviving families to pursue accountability and financial justice for their devastating loss.

🏛️

Civil & Commercial Litigation

Business disputes, contract claims, insurance bad faith, and deceptive trade practices. We handle complex civil matters through trial if necessary.

🏠

Real Estate

Residential and commercial real estate transactions, title work, contract review, and closings through our in-house title company division.

📜

Wills and Probate

Comprehensive estate planning services including wills, testamentary trusts, powers of attorney, medical directives, and probate administration -- thoughtfully structured to protect your family and preserve your legacy.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Law

Simple, uncontested divorces handled with care and efficiency. Please note: we do not handle child custody or child support matters.

🏢

Business & Entity Formation

LLCs, corporations, and partnership agreements. We help entrepreneurs start and structure their businesses on a solid legal foundation.

🛡️

Insurance Bad Faith

When insurers wrongfully deny or delay claims, we fight back. Deceptive trade practice claims and bad faith insurance litigation.
